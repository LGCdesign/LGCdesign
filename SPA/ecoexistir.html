EcoexistirBuilding healthier communities
Speculative urban agriculture service design

The project objective is propose service design innovation for increase life quality of Ñuñoa's population, according to Ñuñoa's people needs and desires.
A different visual brand identity for summer, autumn, winter and spring.
For the development of the business model around Ecoexistir, the "Social business canvas" model is used. This means that Ecoexistir is recognized as a business model with a foundational focus on generating positive impact for communities. Directly identifying the positive impact and beneficiaries that Ecoexistir can have on a community.
Within the interaction map, several key partnerships are recognized. Among them, there is the possibility of involving the government as a promoter. This means proposing the creation of a "vegetal city" as part of the country's public policies.
