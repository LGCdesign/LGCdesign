Vive la CalleCultural community service design

Ñuñoa is a district in the city of Santiago, Chile, primarily composed of middle and upper-middle socioeconomic groups.

The project Vive la Calle was developed along the Ñuñoa's City Hall.
The project objective is propose service design innovation for increase life quality of Ñuñoa's population, according to Ñuñoa's people needs and desires.
