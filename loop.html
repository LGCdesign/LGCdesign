LoopProduct design

Industrialization and advancements in a wide range of fields have led to significant progress in countries. However, they also produce various forms of pollution that severely impact the environment.


Loop is inspired by the aesthetic of the music video "VETE" by Bad Bunny. With its sculptural form and dynamic design, it aims to express the energy, speed, and camera movements that characterize the music video.
To open the compartment, press it; it will release and open smoothly. Gently insert the cage capsule here, then gently press to close.
